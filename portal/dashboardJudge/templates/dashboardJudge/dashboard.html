Guidelines for scoring:

1. Go to Participants option on the left side menu.

2. Press Judge button on the startup to start scoring.

3. On the judging pane, on left side, ppt of the startup is presented, you can use arrow keys below ppt to move slide forward or backward.

4. On the judging pane, on right side there are total 9 parameters, click the '>' arrow to go to next parameters.

5. Move the slider to score.

6. Once you have done scoring of all parameters, click the submit button.

7. Once submitted, you'll go back to Participants section to judge the next startup.

Guidelines for judging:

1. Ask insightful questions to gauge the depth of the startup's understanding of its market and challenges. Also, kindly encourage open and candid responses during Q&A sessions.

2. Adhere to the allocated time for each startup's presentation and Q&A (10+5 minutes respectively). This ensures a fair and consistent evaluation process for all participants.

3. Collaborate with fellow judges to ensure a comprehensive evaluation. You can discuss differing opinions to arrive at a fair and well-rounded decision.
